WaterBuddy Basic


The WaterBuddy Basic is there for those that just need a reminder that their plant needs water.
This is all done trough the smart monitor that you put in your plant's pot, which will monitor the hydration needs.
So, if your plant is in need of water, the WaterBuddy basic will send a notification to your phone through the app.
The WaterBuddy Basic is there to help you keep your plants alive!

WaterBuddy Pro


The WaterBuddy Pro does the same as the WaterBuddy Basic, but we've added a reservoir to automatically water your plants!
Once this reservoir is running low, you'll receive a notification on your mobile through the app reminding you to fill it.
The WaterBuddy Pro is also capable of feeding your plants when needed, thanks to smart monitoring.
With the WaterBuddy Pro you'll never have to worry about keeping your plants alive again!
